Usage
  • Authenticate using your credentials
  • You can subscribe to an Auth subject with window.sdk.Auth$.subscribe(data =>{})
  • Open your browser console and use the sdk with window.sdk.Crossbar.{{module}}.{{method}}, e.g.: await window.sdk.Crossbar.accounts.GetAbout();
  • You can sign out with window.sdk.signOut();
